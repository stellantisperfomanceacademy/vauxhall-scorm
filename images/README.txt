VEHICLE IMAGES — drop your photos here
======================================

The course shows a branded placeholder for each vehicle until you add a photo.
To add a real image, save a JPG with the exact filename below into this folder
(images/). Landscape, roughly 1200x600px or wider works best.

Cars:
  corsa.jpg
  frontera.jpg
  mokka.jpg
  astra.jpg
  grandland.jpg

Vans:
  combo.jpg
  vivaro.jpg
  movano.jpg

Retired models:
  crossland.jpg
  insignia.jpg

After adding images, re-zip the package (see README.md) before uploading to your LMS.
If you prefer PNG, change the extension in js/content.js (the "image" field) — or
ask and I can switch the loader to try .png automatically.
